üí° Program Overview

This program targets the four muscle groups crucial for core stability and back pain relief:

  • Deep Abdominals: Your natural "corset" that stabilizes the spine
  • Spinal Extensors: Keep you upright and control each vertebra
  • Hip & Gluteal Complex: Stable base that takes load off your spine
  • Diaphragm & Pelvic Floor: Vertical stabilization system

üéØ Key Principles

  • Quality over quantity: Perfect form beats more reps
  • Breathe consistently: Never hold your breath
  • Progress gradually: Master each exercise before advancing
  • Stop if pain increases: Mild discomfort OK, sharp pain is not
  • Be consistent: Daily practice yields best results

üö® When to Stop

Stop immediately and consult a healthcare provider if you experience:

  • Sharp or shooting pain
  • Pain that radiates down your leg
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Loss of bladder/bowel control
  • Dizziness or difficulty breathing
